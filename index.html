VITALIS

RPG Adventure

How to Play

Controls

Movement: Arrow Keys or WASD

Attack: Click on enemies or press Space

Spells: F = Fireball, L = Lightning

Inventory: I key or Inventory button

Gameplay

Defeat enemies to gain experience and loot. Collect items and equipment to become stronger. Find stronger enemies as you progress.

Tips

• Use spells to damage multiple enemies at once

• Equip better gear to increase your stats

• Watch your health and heal when needed

• Explore different areas to find rare items
